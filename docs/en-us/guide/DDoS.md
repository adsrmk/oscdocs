# DDoS Protection

A **DDoS (distributed denial-of-service)** attack floods your server with thousands of fake requests, exhausting its bandwidth and resources. The result: legitimate visitors can't reach your site, or only with severe delays. These attacks are typically powered by large networks of compromised computers (botnets).

Our DDoS protection works in three layers:

## 1. Attack pattern recognition

Instead of just monitoring traffic volume, the system analyses incoming traffic to identify the **type** of attack — so it can respond with the right countermeasure.

## 2. Traffic filtering

Once an attack is detected, malicious traffic is routed through scrubbing filters that neutralise common threats such as:

- DNS reflection
- NTP reflection
- UDP floods

Only legitimate traffic reaches your server.

## 3. Challenge-response & dynamic filtering

For more advanced attacks — like SYN floods, DNS floods, and invalid packets — the system adapts in real time. New and unusual threats are mitigated automatically, without disrupting your real visitors.
