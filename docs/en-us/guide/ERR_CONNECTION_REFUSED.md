# ERR_CONNECTION_REFUSED

This error means the server actively refused your connection — often due to security settings, or because the server doesn't exist.

It can also be caused locally by your VPN, proxy, antivirus, or firewall blocking the website or port.

<br>

## How to fix it

**Temporarily disable your VPN, proxy, antivirus, or firewall** and try again. If the site loads, one of those services was blocking the connection — adjust its settings to allow the website.
