# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account. In addition to your password, a second verification step is required to log in, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access.

<br>

**How to set up 2FA**

1. Go to your **Account Settings** in the dashboard.
2. Navigate to the **Security** section and look for **Two-factor authentication**.
3. Scan the QR code using an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) on your phone.
4. Enter the verification code generated by the app to complete the setup.

::: tip
Store your backup codes in a safe, offline location. If you lose access to your authenticator app, these codes are the only way to regain access to your account.
:::
