# Mailbox

A mailbox lets you send, receive, and manage email messages tied to your domain.

## Create a mailbox

1. Go to the [Emails↗](https://oscloud.nl/emails) section in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Account** in the top-right corner.
3. Choose your email address and domain.
4. Select an account type.
5. Set the mailbox size (maximum **1 GB**).
6. Create a username and password.

<div class="tip custom-block" style="padding-top: 8px">
A 1 GB mailbox is enough for thousands of emails without attachments. The <b>Essential Plan</b> includes 3 mailboxes; higher plans include more. If your business frequently sends or receives large files, we recommend upgrading to Google Workspace or ProtonMail for additional storage and advanced features.
</div>

## Change the password

1. Go to your mailbox in the [Emails↗](https://oscloud.nl/emails) section and click **Reset**.
2. Enter your new password. It must meet these requirements:
   - At least one lowercase letter
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character
   - Minimum of 10 characters
3. Click **Save** to apply the change.

<div class="warning custom-block" style="padding-top: 8px">
If your email is set up on other devices (phone, tablet, mail client), update the password there as well. Otherwise, you'll stop receiving new emails on those devices.
</div>
