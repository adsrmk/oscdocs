# Credentials

After registering for our cloud services, your account will be activated within a few hours. Once activated, you'll have full access to all available services.

You can log in using the **contact email address** and the **password** you set during the registration form.

## Forgot your password?

If you're not sure about your password, you can reset it easily:

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the login button.
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to set a new password.

## Forgot or need to change your login email?

If you've forgotten your login email address — or if you'd like to change it — please contact our support team at [support@originstudio.nl](mailto:support@originstudio.nl) and we'll help you out.
