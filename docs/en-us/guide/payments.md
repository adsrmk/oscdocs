# Payment Methods

We support **Debit/Credit Cards**, **SEPA Direct Debit**, and **iDEAL** via [Mollie](https://www.mollie.com/).

All payments are processed securely through Mollie. Depending on the service, payments are handled automatically or via a payment link:

- **Subscriptions** — charged automatically (if SEPA Direct Debit is active)
- **Invoices** — paid via a secure payment link (iDEAL, Card, or SEPA)

<div class="info custom-block" style="padding-top: 8px">
Recurring charges are processed on the <b>first day of each month</b>.
</div>

<br>

## How payments work

After placing an order or receiving an invoice:

1. You'll receive a payment link by email.
2. Choose your preferred payment method (iDEAL, Card, or SEPA Direct Debit).
3. Complete the payment securely via Mollie.

For subscriptions paid via SEPA Direct Debit, future payments are collected automatically — no further action needed.

<br>

## SEPA Direct Debit (automatic payments)

When you choose SEPA Direct Debit:

- You authorise automatic recurring payments.
- Future invoices are paid automatically.
- No manual action is required.
